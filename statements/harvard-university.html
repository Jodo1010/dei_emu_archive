Harvard University

Archived: February 15, 2025 University Original URL: https://example.com/harvard-dei

Harvard University is committed to fostering a campus environment that is diverse, equitable, and inclusive. We believe that an inclusive learning environment enriches the academic experience for all members of our community and prepares our students to thrive as citizens and leaders in a diverse society.

Our commitment to diversity, equity, and inclusion is grounded in the University's mission to educate the citizens and citizen-leaders for our society. It is central to the University's educational philosophy, rooted in the conviction that our learning environment is enhanced when students have the opportunity to engage with peers from different backgrounds, perspectives, and experiences.

Our Principles

Harvard strives to create an environment characterized by:

  • Respect for All: Every member of our community deserves to be treated with dignity and respect.
  • Inclusive Excellence: We recognize that excellence and diversity are inextricably linked.
  • Equal Opportunity: We are committed to providing equal opportunity for all community members to thrive.
  • Diversity of Thought: We value the diverse perspectives and experiences that each individual brings.

Our Commitments

Harvard University is dedicated to:

  • Recruiting and retaining diverse students, faculty, and staff
  • Fostering an inclusive campus climate where all feel welcome, respected, and valued
  • Incorporating diverse perspectives into our curriculum and research
  • Advancing knowledge and research on issues of equity and inclusion
  • Engaging with our neighboring communities in ways that respect their diversity and promote mutual benefit

We recognize that building a truly inclusive community is an ongoing process that requires continuous commitment, reflection, and action. We are committed to this work and to holding ourselves accountable for progress toward our goals.
